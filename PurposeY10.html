Purpose of this GCP

Engaging Community

In Year 10, students will immerse in community engagement activities by partnering with different NGOs and organizations dedicated to social good. With the knowledge they obtained in previous GCP years (society, culture & nature), students are now to look deeply at different issues present in society, analyze, brainstorm, and give their own solutions to these problems.
