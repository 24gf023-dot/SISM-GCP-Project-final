Coordinator profile

Erjo Olivares Coscolluela

Teacher Erjo hails from Baguio City in the Philippines, a mountain resort city known as the “City of Pines.” He holds a degree in International Relations from Sogang University in South Korea.

In addition to leading SISMs Global Citizenship Programme, he is deeply passionate about social impact and community development. He also serves as a board member and Lead for Monitoring and Evaluation for the KOMUNIDA Initiative, a youth-based NGO in the Philippines.

Teacher Erjo is passionate about learning about the world—its cultures, people, and pressing global issues. He believes that the first step to becoming a global citizen is cultivating a natural curiosity to explore and celebrate diverse cultures. He hopes that all SISM students will discover the joy of becoming global citizens and future leaders.
