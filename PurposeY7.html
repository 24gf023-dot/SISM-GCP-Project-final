Purpose of Y7 GCP

Building Friendship

Upon entering the school at Year 7, students are welcomed into a new environment that may seem intimidating and scary. However, meeting new people & making new friends can help to ease their worries by offering a sense of camaraderie, togetherness, and belonging. The school camp aims to establish these bonds, as well as give students a better understanding of their own identity amongst their new peers.
